How to play the game

The aim of the game is to pass through each barrier that comes your way. For every barrier that you successfully pass through, your score will increase by 1 and along with this, so will your speed.

Do you have what it takes to keep going as the speed continues to increase?

By pressing the 'spacebar' the bird will rise up, but note this is temporary and the bird will start to descend, so you need to maintain its height as you pass through the barriers.

Good Luck !
